## Scenario Evidence Mapping

### S2-S7 (DDoS Scenarios)
- CERT-In confirms Indian smart cities are active botnet targets
- India DDoS attacks up 50% in 2024 (India Times)
- Financial sector (BKC) was primary target in 2024

### M1 (IoT Botnet Recruitment)
- CERT-In/Kaspersky 2025: specifically identified SNMP-exploitable devices in Indian smart city controllers
- Mirai botnet (2016-present): IoT botnets exploit default credentials globally

### M2 (Hospital Ransomware)
- AIIMS Delhi 2022: Real precedent for Indian hospital cyberattack
- Healthcare sector globally under ransomware epidemic
- Smart lighting in hospitals controls emergency lighting, surgical suite brightness

### M3 (Firmware Rootkit on Airport)
- Mumbai Power Grid 2020: demonstrates state-level targeting of Indian critical infrastructure
- Airport lighting = national infrastructure (IEC 62443 SL-4)

### M4 (Cryptominer on Residential)
- Growing trend of cryptojacking IoT devices globally
- Residential devices have weakest security (SL-2)
- Low-priority, often unmonitored
